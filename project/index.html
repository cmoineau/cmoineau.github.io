Ada Lovelace & Hacakthon ENSSAT (2019)

During my 2nd year at the ENSSAT, I worked on two sides project, with the same team of student. These projects where about programming challenge for students. You will find above a description of both project, followed by my participation in them.

Description of the projet Ada Lovelace :

The challenge Ada Lovelace is an computer science challenge where girls from high school have to create a web site around a topic in a group of 4-5. This is project aim to promote the place of women in the numeric world, which is often consider to be man only has shown by statistics :

There is not even 1/3 of women in engineer formation (first line). In order to change things, the Lycée Le Dantec, the ENSSAT, Orange and Elles Bougent decide to create this event to touch young girls and give them a good first experience of the numeric world.

To achieve this goal, groups are helped by women who are in higher study, this help is mandatory since the schoolgirl have no notion in web. But the tutor also give them an example of women that work in the numeric, which can break stereotypes.

Description of the projet Hackathon ENSSAT :

The Hackathon ENSSAT, is a programming competition, open for students. During this event, the participants have to propose a solution around a thematic, in a limited time of 24 hours. They are in a team of 5 and have to make a small presentation at the end of the 24th hour. The students are evaluated by a professional jury, both from the indistrual and laboratory field. The goal of this competition is to help student improve themselves by putting them in an extrem situation ("only" 24 hours). And to give them a good moment arround their passion for programming.

Projet BomberBall

The company wanted to create a video game similar to the Bomber Man.
