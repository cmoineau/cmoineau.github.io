Internship at the NCTU 2019

Description of the internship :

During the summer 2019, I spend 2 months at the National Chiao Tung University (NCTU) in the Intelligent Electronics and System-On-Chip (IESOC) laboratory.

During this 2 months, I have worked on detecting emotion using ECG (Electrocardiogram) and PPG (Photoplethysmogram) signals. For this I used CNN (convolutional neuronnal network) with the library Keras (v2.24). This also made me work on problematic such as peak detection and real time implementation. Indeed, at the end of the internship I had to implement my CNN on the JetSon Nano board.

With the result I have acquired, I have been proposed to write an article for the ISCAS conference, the paper is currently under redaction.

Conclusion :

This internship was a very good first experience of the world of research. I can't thanks enought the people that welcome me at the NCTU and made this two months such a good experience. I have been able to understand difficulties of working in a research laboratory. Even if the thematic was not on the scope of what I have done at school, I was really eager to learn. This two months definetly confirm my desire to do research.

Orléans Métropole 2018

Description of the internship :

I spend 2 months during the summer 2018 at the Orléans Métropole at the Information Systems Division (ISD). During this summer, I work on two different topics.

Establishement of a supervision using the OS Centreon. I arrived during the fusion between the services of every ISD of the metropole. In this contexte, the ISD of Orléans decided to upgrade their supervision services. During this internship I have been able to witness a real network. I have learned many things about network and virtualization.

The other topic was the management of the Active Directory (AD). Due to some bad practice, the AD has a lot of user who are supposed to be gone. This project was really interresting since I had to create a way to detect users who aren't supposed to have an active directory with the information given by the AD. For this I used Power Shell script.

Conclusion :

This was my first technical internship. I learned a lot, and I had the opportunity of been given responsabilities. Especially for the cleaning of the AD, where a mistake could have lead to the supression of the account of someone working.
